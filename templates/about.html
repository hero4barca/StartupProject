About Us

Market woman is an online grocery delivery service that covers the city of Port-Harcourt. We offer fresh groceries at a price that is very affordable and comparable to any of Port-Harcourt's numerous open markets. Market Woman aims to make grocery shopping easier, faster and more convenient for you while keeping the prices affordable. We promise reliable service delivery and top quality customer service to all customers.

Our deliveries could be as early as the very next day excluding sundays. However, we do not make deliveries on the same day an order is made (i.e. to have you order delivered by tomorrow, you have to order by tonight at the latest).

Delivery hours are between 10am and 4pm.
